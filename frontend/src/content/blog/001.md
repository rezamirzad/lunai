The landscape of venture-backed startups is changing. It's no longer enough to simply automate repetitive tasks. We are entering an era of **agentic frameworks**.

At **LUMZ**, we are deploying specialized agents that act as autonomous extensions of your core team—handling everything from backend architecture to cross-border marketing strategy.

The shift is simple:

1. **From Tools to Agents:** Moving from software that requires manual input to agents that understand intent.
2. **From Execution to Strategy:** Allowing founders to focus on the vision while agents handle the implementation.
